## Who I Am

I'm an AI Software Solutions Engineer at **Intel India** with 5+ years of experience in GPU software development, AI frameworks, and graphics systems. My work sits at the intersection of deep learning, compiler infrastructure, and GPU hardware — building the low-level foundations that make large-scale AI workloads fast.

I specialize in **PyTorch GPU extension development**, kernel enablement via SYCL C++ and Triton backends, and AI accelerator/GPU integration. I've contributed to Intel's driver stack (DirectML, DX12, UMD), deployed large video AI models on Intel GPUs, and led research spanning AR/VR, vision transformers, and educational AI tools.
